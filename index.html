About the Owner

Meet Jamie Russell

Jamie Russell has over 12 years of experience in the oil and gas industry. She began her career with a midstream contractor, spending nine years in pipeline construction, facilities installation, and industrial insulation before taking ownership of the insulation division when the company downsized.

Jamie built a strong reputation for exceptional insulation services while serving clients including Devon Energy, EOG Resources, and Targa Resources. Her commitment to high-quality solutions is matched by a focus on fair pricing, customer service, and creating a rewarding environment for her dedicated team.

With the rise in demand following the 2022 Rule 3.66 Weather Emergency Preparedness Standards, Jamie is focused on growing the team while fostering a culture of honesty, integrity, family, safety, and excellence. She holds a Bachelor of Science from East Texas State University.
